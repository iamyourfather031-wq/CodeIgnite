Mission and Vision

Mission

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an accessible and interactive learning platform that helps students develop basic web development skills and gain confidence in learning HTML.

Vision

Our Vision

Our vision is to empower students with digital skills by making web development education engaging, effective, and accessible to all learners.

What is CodeIgnite?

CodeIgnite helps students learn HTML and web development through simple and interactive lessons.

CodeIgnite is a learning platform designed to teach basic HTML in an easy and engaging way for beginners.

This website aims to improve students web development skills by providing hands-on HTML practice.
